School

My school life began at a place called Rye Country Day. I attended for both preschool and kindergarten. I then moved onto 1st-8th grade, which took place at North Hampton Middle School. This is where my sports career began and where I got my foundational schooling done.

In 2011, I began high school at my towns public school, Winnacunnet High School. I only attended for a year there. I then transferred to a private school about 30 minutes from my home town called Saint Thomas Aquinas High School. I graduated from there in 2015 as a three sport student athlete.

After Saint Thomas, I attended Plymouth State University for two semesters. I then transferred to Great Bay Community College for my next two semesters. I'm currently onto my third and final college, the University of Maine. It took me three tries but I finally found the perfect school. I plan to graduate in 2019 with a double major in Finance and Management.
